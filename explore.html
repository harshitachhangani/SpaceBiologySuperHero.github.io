Explore, Imagine & make it Reality

Since the beginning of time, exploring the Universe has been a dream of humankind. Human curiosity has fuelled interest in exploring and discovering new worlds, pushing the boundaries of the known, and expanding scientific and technical knowledge.

Let's explore some intresting topics...

Black Hole

A black hole is a place in space where gravity pulls so much that even light can not get out. The gravity is so strong because matter has been squeezed into a tiny space. This can happen when a star is dying..

Multiverse

The multiverse is a term that scientists use to describe the idea that beyond the observable universe, other universes may exist as well.The multiverse is a hypothetical group of multiple universes.

Interstellar Travel

Interstellar travel refers to the idea of interstellar probes or crewed spacecraft moving between stars or planetary systems in a galaxy. Interstellar travel would be much more difficult than interplanetary spaceflight.

Big Bang Theory

The Big Bang theory describes how the universe expanded from an initial state of high density and temperature.Most astronomers use the Big Bang theory to explain how the universe began.

Some Space Organizations

ISRO

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is the national space agency of India, headquartered in Bangalore.It has sent two missions to the Moon and one to Mars.

NASA

The National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) is an independent agency of the US federal government responsible for the civil space program, aeronautics & space research.

RFSA

The State Space Corporation "Roscosmos", is a state corporation of the Russian Federation responsible for space flights, cosmonautics programs, and aerospace research.

JAXA

The Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency (JAXA) ("National Research and Development Agency on Aerospace Research and Development") is the Japanese national air and space agency.
